1. How to generate bill ?

  1. Select the item.
  2. Put the quantity.
  3. Click add button to add in list.
  4. Click the Generate bill buttom
  5. Finaly you can take print out the bill by clicking Ctrl+P.

2. How to add, edit and delete items on Inventory?

  1. Click Inventory button.
  2. Click Add button.
  3. Enter Item Name.
  4. Enter the price of the item.
  5. Finaly click submit.
  6. Click ✎ to edit items.
  7. Click ✖ to delete items.
